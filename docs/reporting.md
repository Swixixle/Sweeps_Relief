# Reporting and evidence

## Principles

- Default is **not** to email third parties on every block event.
- Prefer **signed daily/weekly summaries**, relapse-pressure digests, tamper-attempt digests, and **evidence packet export** when *you* choose to involve attorneys, journalists, counsel, or legislators.

## Formats

- JSON and CSV-friendly structures in code (Markdown reports via `build-report`; PDF later).

## Bundles

- `LogBundle`: signs a digest of ordered events for a period—useful for accountability partners or archival, not impulsive broadcast.

## Privacy and credibility

- Raw automated complaint spam undermines credibility and creates privacy risk.
- Human-reviewed or scheduled opt-in digests preserve intent and proportionality.
